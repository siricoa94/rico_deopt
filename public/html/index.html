Signing Up

Rico Depot is a "mock" market place, as in no real money is used and no actual products can be purchased. This is very much like a game, but just like you would expect a real application to properly keep track of customers, products, and purchases, so does Rico Depot!

To get started is easy fast, and does not require you to enter in any real credentials. Please do not enter a real phone number or address into these fields, just like a game, think of your profile as a character. You can use a real email if you would like, but again you also have the option to make a fake one in this case.

An example of a good fake account could be the following:

  • First Name: "Andrew"
  • Last Name: "Sirico"
  • Email Address: "Depot123@fakemail.com"
  • Street Address: "12 Rico Way"
  • Phone Number: "123-456-7890"
  • Password: "SomethingYouRemember"

Upon creating an acount you will automatically be given credits to make purchases, and the order history of purchases can be found inside the application.
